My journey...


I hail from New Delhi, India, Currently working at a start-up as Deep Learning Engineer.

My interest in Artificial Intelligence arose after the news of Alpha Go defeating one of the best players in the world went viral. It was so fascinating that we are in an age where Machines have started beating the human brain.

I started doing some courses such as the one offered by Andrew ng on Coursera, but after some time, I got overwhelmed with the vast amount of multidisciplinary knowledge required and eventually gave up on it.

Yet who knew that my path would lead back to DL again after I stumbled across the cs231n course offered by Stanford led by the legendary Andrej Karpathy. And this time, I had a better background knowledge which helped me motivated to keep going.

I am currently working as a DL Engineer while also working on personal projects as well as participating in Kaggle competitions.
