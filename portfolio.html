Bio.

My journey...


I hail from New Delhi, India, Currently working at a start-up as Deep Learning Engineer.

My interest in Artificial Intelligence arose after the news of Alpha Go defeating one of the best Go players in the world went viral. It was so fascinating that we are in an age where Machines have started defeating the human brain.

This encouraged me to join some online courses, like the one by Andrew NG on Coursera. I was a freshmen back then. The vast amount of multidisciplinary knowledge required was overwhelming and eventually I gave up on it.

Yet, who knew that my path would lead back to DL after I stumbled across the cs231n course offered by Stanford led by the legendary Andrej Karpathy. My interest rekindled, and this time, the mathematical background from my engineering course allowed me to not only complete the course but also start my own projects.

Now, I am a Engineer and also participate in Kaggle competitions.
