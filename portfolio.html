My journey...


I hail from New Delhi, India. Currently working at a start-up as Deep Learning Engineer.

My interest in Artificial Intelligence arose after the news of Alpha Go defeating one of the best players in the world went viral. It was so fascinating that we are in an age where Machines have started defeating human brain.

I started doing some courses such as the one offered by Andrew ng on coursera but after some time I got overwhelmed with vast amount of multidisciplinary knowledge required and eventually gave up on it.

Yet as it turns out, my path would lead back to DL again after I stumbled across the cs231n course offered by stanford led by the legendary Andrej Karpathy. And this time I had a better background knowledge which helped me motivated to keep going.

I am now working as a DL Engineer while also working on personal projects as well as participating in Kaggle competitions.
